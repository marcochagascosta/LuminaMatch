## Full description

Restore the **Palace of Light** by matching glowing gems.

Lumina Match is a fast casual match-3:
• 60 levels with rising challenge
• Collect gems, clear ice and boxes
• Boosters: hammer, swap, line blast
• Lives that refill over time
• Build your palace piece by piece

Play a few minutes or a long session — always one more level.

Free to play. Optional in-app purchases for coins, lives and boosters.

# Privacy Policy — Lumina Match

**Last updated:** 2026-07-16  
**Developer:** MarcoSaas / Marco Costa  
**Contact:** Use the support email configured in App Store Connect / Google Play Console for Lumina Match.

## Summary

Lumina Match is a casual match-3 game. Progress (lives, coins, boosters, levels) is stored **locally on your device**. This MVP does not require an account.

## Data we process

### On device
- Game progress and settings (via local storage / PlayerPrefs)
- Optional purchase receipts processed by Apple / Google when you buy in-app items

### Not collected by us in this MVP
- No custom analytics backend
- No account registration
- No precise location tracking
- No contact list access

### Third parties (when enabled in a store build)
- **Apple App Store / Google Play** process payments
- If ads are enabled later (Unity Ads / AdMob), those SDKs may collect advertising identifiers according to their own policies

## Children

The game is intended for a general audience (puzzle). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parental guidance applies for in-app purchases.

## Your choices

- You can delete the app to remove local progress
- You can manage purchases and subscriptions in your Apple ID / Google account settings
- Device advertising / tracking settings are controlled by iOS / Android system settings

## Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change when we do.

## Contact

For privacy questions about Lumina Match, contact the developer through the store listing support channel.
